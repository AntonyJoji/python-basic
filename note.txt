data science :
              Data Science is the process of using data (information) to find answers and make better decisions.

               It combines:
              Mathematics & Statistics → to understand numbers.
              Programming (Python, R, SQL, etc.) → to work with data.
             Business/Domain knowledge → to solve real-world problems.
                 example: recomentation,self driving car 
                 Collect raw data (from websites, apps, sensors, etc.)
                 Clean and organize it.
                 Analyze it with algorithms and models.
                 Find patterns, trends, and predictions.
